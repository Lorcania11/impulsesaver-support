How do I create my first savings goal?

Tap the "Desires" tab, then the "+" button. Enter what you want to save for, target amount, and how long you want to lock the money away.

How do I save money when I'm tempted?

Go to the "Deposit" tab, select your goal, enter the amount you would have spent, then upload a screenshot showing you transferred that money to your savings account.

üîí How It Works

What happens to my money?

You transfer money to your own bank savings account. The app tracks your commitment but doesn't hold your money - you stay in control of your funds at all times.

Can I access locked money early?

The app encourages you to wait until your commitment period ends, but since the money is in your own account, you have ultimate control.

üîê Privacy & Security

Do you access my bank account?

No! We never connect to or access your bank accounts. You simply upload screenshots as proof of your savings commitment.

What happens to my screenshots?

Screenshots are stored locally on your device. We don't upload or store your financial information on our servers.
